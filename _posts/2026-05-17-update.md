## Release Schedule

**Kubernetes v1.37 Release Cycle Kicks Off, 18th May 2026**

The [v1.37 release schedule](https://github.com/kubernetes/sig-release/pull/3016/) has been posted, with the release cycle beginning May 18.

Applications for the Kubernetes v1.37 Release Team shadow program closed on May 15, with selected applicants announced on May 22.

Kubernetes Patches [v1.33.12](https://git.k8s.io/kubernetes/CHANGELOG/CHANGELOG-1.33.md), [v1.34.8](https://github.com/kubernetes/kubernetes/blob/master/CHANGELOG/CHANGELOG-1.34.md), [v1.35.5](https://git.k8s.io/kubernetes/CHANGELOG/CHANGELOG-1.35.md), and [v1.36.1](https://git.k8s.io/kubernetes/CHANGELOG/CHANGELOG-1.36.md) have been released.
