## Developer News
[Registration](https://groups.google.com/a/kubernetes.io/g/dev/c/R7JA1NXdTyk) for KubeCon NA 2026 Project Pavilion is now underway, with SIG ContribEx coordinating participation and onsite PR support for Kubernetes project groups. Interested SIGs, WGs, Committees, and Subprojects can [register](https://forms.gle/RXHyhtPtkcfLzePdA) by July 19.

Next Tuesday's monthly [New Contributor Orientation](https://www.kubernetes.dev/docs/orientation/) features a SIG Docs-focused session. As the standalone SIG Docs New Contributor Orientation winds down, documentation-focused onboarding will continue through the monthly NCO. Join the [session](https://www.kubernetes.dev/resources/calendar/) to learn how to get started contributing to Kubernetes documentation and how you can get involved with SIG Docs.

Kubernetes v1.37 enters Enhancements Freeze on June 17th. Ensure all enhancement tracking issues, KEP requirements, and Production Readiness Review requirements are completed before the deadline.
