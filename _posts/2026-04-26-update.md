## Developer News

The AI Conformance subproject has [moved to the SIG Architecture mailing list](https://groups.google.com/a/kubernetes.io/g/dev/c/bOUBzX4O7HA); contributors should join [sig-architecture@kubernetes.io](https://groups.google.com/a/kubernetes.io/g/sig-architecture) for future AI Conformance meeting invites and announcements.

There is an [active discussion on the AI usage policy's interaction with GitHub Copilot and CLA mechanics](https://groups.google.com/a/kubernetes.io/g/steering/c/lyKZlNtt93U); contributors using Copilot-generated commits should review the thread before submitting PRs.
