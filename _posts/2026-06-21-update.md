## Developer News
A proposal has been submitted to establish [WG Node Identity](https://groups.google.com/a/kubernetes.io/g/dev/c/Blxf5Vu22Kk), a new Working Group intended to coordinate cross-SIG efforts around secure node identity. The effort aims to develop a common approach to hardware-backed node attestation and node identity verification in Kubernetes.

The 2026 Kubernetes [Steering Committee election](https://groups.google.com/a/kubernetes.io/g/dev/c/_yL6ZhQcE_w) season has begun, with nominations now open for three Steering Committee seats, each serving a two-year term. The first phase of the election also includes voter exception requests.

Mario Fahlandt has been nominated to serve as an additional [SIG ContribEx Co-Chair](https://groups.google.com/a/kubernetes.io/g/dev/c/lSlmpkJiZVw). He has contributed across several community initiatives, including Contributor Summits, New Contributor Orientation, and contributor outreach efforts. The leadership team is being expanded to help share the growing responsibilities of SIG ContribEx.

After serving as [co-chair of Kubernetes SIG Storage](https://groups.google.com/a/kubernetes.io/g/dev/c/3fKiL3P_LPA) for over 10 years, Saad has announced that he will be stepping down from the role. Hemant Kumar has been nominated as the new co-chair, bringing long-term involvement with the SIG and a strong understanding of its goals and direction.

[Registration](https://forms.gle/RXHyhtPtkcfLzePdA) is now open for the [KubeCon NA 2026 Project Pavilion and onsite PR support program.](https://groups.google.com/a/kubernetes.io/g/dev/c/ClmM5_l6xVU) SIGs, WGs, Committees, and Subprojects planning to showcase their work or announce major updates at KubeCon can apply for participation and communications support through July 19, 2026.
