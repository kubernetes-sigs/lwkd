## Developer News

The **July Kubernetes patch releases** have been postponed until early next week. As a result, the **cherry-pick deadline has been extended to Friday, July 17, 5:00 PM Pacific Time**, giving contributors additional time to submit cherry picks for review. Questions can be directed to the `#release-management` Slack channel.

[Kubernetes v1.37 Code Freeze and Test Freeze](https://github.com/kubernetes/sig-release/blob/master/releases/release_phases.md#code-freeze) are one week away, taking place on **July 22 (AoE) / July 23, 12:00 UTC**. Contributors targeting v1.37 should ensure KEP-related and major Kubernetes PRs are merged before the deadline. If needed, submit an [exception request](https://github.com/kubernetes/sig-release/blob/master/releases/EXCEPTIONS.md) ahead of time. With **KubeCon Japan 2026** overlapping the documentation review period, contributors are also encouraged to finalize documentation PRs early.

[Registration](https://register.linuxfoundation.org/kccnc-na-maintainer-summit-2026) is now open for **Maintainer Summit North America 2026**, taking place on **November 8** in **Salt Lake City, Utah**, ahead of KubeCon + CloudNativeCon NA. The event is open to eligible Kubernetes and CNCF project maintainers, organization members, and community leaders. Active contributors who are not yet organization members are encouraged to apply.
