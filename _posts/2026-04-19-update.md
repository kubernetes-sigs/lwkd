* [cluster-api v1.13.0-rc.1](https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/cluster-api/releases/tag/v1.13.0-rc.1): fixes CVE-2026-39883, fixes KCP deletion when InfraTemplates are missing, marks CAPD docker resources as deprecated
