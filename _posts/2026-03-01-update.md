## Other Merges

* [Rename `AllowlistEntry.Name`](https://github.com/kubernetes/kubernetes/pull/137272) to `AllowlistEntry.Command` in the credential plugin allowlist.
* Add the ability for [statefulset controller to read its own pod and pvc writes](https://github.com/kubernetes/kubernetes/pull/137254)
* kubeadm: Do not add [learner member to etcd client endpoints](https://github.com/kubernetes/kubernetes/pull/137251)
* [Prevent the replicaset controller from spuriously reconciling](https://github.com/kubernetes/kubernetes/pull/137212) while its own writes have not been read. 
* Add the [ability for the job controller to read its own writes](https://github.com/kubernetes/kubernetes/pull/137210)
* Allow [kube-apiserver to recover](https://github.com/kubernetes/kubernetes/pull/137157) from an accidentally made connection to a wrong server.
* Fixed [apiserver startup failure](https://github.com/kubernetes/kubernetes/pull/137147) during upgrade when MultiCIDRServiceAllocator is enabled and the cluster has a large number of namespaces.
* Fixed /metrics/resource container_swap_usage_bytes to [report the correct container swap usage](https://github.com/kubernetes/kubernetes/pull/137098).
* [Adds alpha metrics `apiserver_peer_proxy_errors_total` and `apiserver_peer_discovery_sync_errors_total` to apiserver](https://github.com/kubernetes/kubernetes/pull/137065) to track errors encountered in peer proxying and peer discovery.
* [Instrument /flagz and /statusz](https://github.com/kubernetes/kubernetes/pull/137021) endpoints with apiserver request metrics (apiserver_request_total, apiserver_request_duration_seconds), with group and version labels reflecting the content-negotiated API version.
